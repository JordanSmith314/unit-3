required education

To be an architect you must complete a professional bachelors or masters degree in architecture. Your degree also must be accredited by the National Architectural Accrediting Board and qualify for a state licensure. Besides college education, you must be creative, analytical, and organized. Highschool graduates can enroll in a bachelor of architecture program which usually takes about five years to complete.pre-professional bachelor degree programs in architecture usually take about 4 years to complete.
